4)Use better ways to travel:Use buses,trains,or bikes instead of cars when you can. Electric cars are better for the enviornment because they don't make the air dirty.

6)Farm in a way that doesn't hurt the enviornment:Use fewer chemicals on plants,and try to grow food in a way that doesn't hurt the soil or the water.
